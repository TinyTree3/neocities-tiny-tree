About Me!

Hello! My name is Tree and I'm a college junior majoring in Computer Engineering in the US. In my free time, I like dabbling in hobbies and never sticking to them for more than a week. Some of my hobbies have included playing guitar and piano, learning French and Spanish, knitting, crocheting, reading, longboarding, archery, and going on late night walks.

I've been interested in making a personal and retro-style website after having nostalgia for Frutiger Aero and older technology in my childhood. I grew up using Windows 7 and I love the glassy, futuristic, and bright vibe it had. This inspired me to make this site with the limited HTML/CSS knowledge that I have. Hopefully I'm able to stick to it and keep it updated for many more years to come. Happy surfing! :)
